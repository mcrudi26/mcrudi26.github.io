About Erin

With a lifelong passion for interior design and a background in fashion merchandising, I've seamlessly transitioned from a 25-year career with Nautica and Perry Ellis to the world of staging and interior design. Graduating from the American College with a BA in Fashion Merchandising, I bring a unique blend of style, emotion, and budget considerations to transform properties into captivating living spaces. From decluttering to fully staged homes, my portfolio showcases a diverse range of talents, consistently resulting in properties under contract within 2 days, often surpassing list prices. Based in Chapel Hill/Durham for over 25 years, I share my life with my husband, Tom, and our two children, Price and Wilson.
